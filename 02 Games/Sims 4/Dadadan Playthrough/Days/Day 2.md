Due to their alternate work schedules [Okarun](../Characters/Ken%20Takakura.md) and [Momo](../Characters/Momo%20Ayase.md) have not been able to spend time as a couple recently. As an apology  [Ken Takakura](../Characters/Ken%20Takakura.md) offered to take [Momo](../Characters/Momo%20Ayase.md) on a date to Nordhaven, as they both have the day off. The choose to go to the Konst Modern Museum.

Well, they catch up until [Momo](../Characters/Momo%20Ayase.md) made the "mistake" of bringing up a local alien sighting.
